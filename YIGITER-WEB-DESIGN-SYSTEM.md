## Visual, Content and Component Standards

# 1. DESIGN GOAL

Create a modern, industrial, trustworthy B2B website for Yiğiter Orman.

The design must communicate:

- Production strength
- Supply continuity
- Export capability
- Professional logistics
- Long-term partnership
- Clean and premium industrial identity

The website should feel:

- Reliable
- Clear
- Technical but not cold
- Modern but not flashy
- B2B sales-oriented
- Export-ready

It should NOT feel:

- Retail-oriented
- Decorative without substance
- Overly corporate and vague
- Like a small local shop
- Like a generic template website

---

# 2. BRAND VOICE

## 2.1 Tone

Use a direct, confident and professional tone.

Preferred tone:

- Clear
- Short
- Practical
- B2B-focused
- Trust-building
- Export-aware

Avoid:

- Long corporate clichés
- Overpromising
- Emotional consumer language
- Very technical wording without explanation
- Generic phrases like “quality is our priority” without proof

---

## 2.2 Messaging Principles

Every important section should answer one of these questions:

1. What does Yiğiter supply?
2. Who does Yiğiter serve?
3. Why should a B2B buyer trust Yiğiter?
4. How can the buyer request a quote?
5. Can Yiğiter support export/distribution?

---

## 2.3 Core Positioning Sentence

Turkish:

> Yiğiter Orman Ürünleri; kapı sanayii için kapı yüzeyi, kasa, pervaz, melamin, panel ve boya çözümleri sunan üretici, distribütör ve ihracat partneridir.

English:

> Yiğiter Orman Products is a manufacturer, distributor and export partner supplying door surfaces, frames, casings, melamine, panels and paint solutions for the door industry.

---

# 3. VISUAL IDENTITY

## 3.1 General Visual Direction

Use real industrial visuals whenever possible:

- Factory exterior
- Production line
- Warehouse
- Product detail
- Loading / logistics
- Operators working
- Packaged products

Avoid:

- Random stock photos
- Empty warehouse images
- Dark, dirty or cluttered photos
- Excessive filters
- Overly artificial AI visuals

---

## 3.2 Photo Style

Photos should be:

- Clean
- Bright
- Straightened
- High-resolution
- Web-optimized
- Slightly warm but natural
- Industrial and premium

Editing rules:

- Remove distracting objects where possible
- Correct exposure and white balance
- Keep factory reality, do not make it fake
- Keep Yiğiter branding visible when useful
- Avoid heavy blur except for background emphasis

---

## 3.3 Recommended Image Types by Page

Homepage:

- Factory / production / shipment hero
- Product group visuals
- Trust-building production images

Product pages:

- Clean product photos
- Detail close-ups
- Technical or usage context images

Export page:

- Packaging
- Pallets
- Container loading
- Warehouse/logistics
- Product groups ready for export

Production page:

- Machines
- Operators
- Raw material flow
- Finished product flow

---

# 4. COLOR SYSTEM

## 4.1 Direction

The color system should feel industrial, natural and premium.

Recommended palette direction:

- Deep forest green / corporate green
- Warm wood tones
- Clean white backgrounds
- Light neutral gray
- Dark charcoal text

Suggested usage:

- Green: primary brand and CTA accent
- White: main page background
- Light gray: section separation
- Charcoal: text and headings
- Wood tones: visual warmth, not dominant UI color

Important:

Use exact brand colors from Yiğiter logo if available.

---

## 4.2 Color Usage Rules

- CTA buttons must be visually strong.
- Do not use too many accent colors.
- Keep backgrounds clean.
- Avoid dark text on busy images.
- Use overlays on hero images if text readability is weak.

---

# 5. TYPOGRAPHY

## 5.1 Font Direction

Recommended style:

- Modern sans-serif
- Clean Turkish character support
- Good readability on mobile
- Professional B2B feeling

Possible font categories:

- Inter-like
- Manrope-like
- Source Sans-like
- IBM Plex Sans-like

Final font must support Turkish characters correctly:

- ğ
- ü
- ş
- ı
- ö
- ç
- İ

---

## 5.2 Type Scale

Suggested hierarchy:

- H1: strong, short, clear
- H2: section title
- H3: card/product title
- Body: readable, not small
- Caption: only for metadata/specs

Rules:

- Avoid long H1 titles.
- Body text should be short and scannable.
- Product specs should be formatted in tables or bullet groups.

---

# 6. SPACING AND LAYOUT

## 6.1 Layout Direction

Use a clean grid-based layout.

Recommended structure:

- Wide desktop container
- Comfortable white space
- Clear section separation
- Strong mobile stacking
- CTA visible in each major section

Avoid:

- Dense text blocks
- Too many cards in one row
- Misaligned product cards
- Inconsistent spacing
- Footer clutter

---

## 6.2 Section Order — Homepage

Recommended homepage order:

1. Header / Navigation
2. Hero
3. Product groups
4. Why Yiğiter
5. Production capability
6. Export capability
7. Trust / metrics
8. Request quote CTA
9. Footer

---

# 7. COMPONENT STANDARDS

## 7.1 Header

Header must include:

- Logo
- Main navigation
- Request Quote CTA
- Language switch if English structure is active

Desktop behavior:

- Clean horizontal menu
- CTA on right

Mobile behavior:

- Menu icon
- Sticky or easily reachable quote/WhatsApp action

---

## 7.2 Hero Component

Hero must include:

- Clear H1
- Short subtitle
- Primary CTA
- Secondary CTA
- Strong real visual

Recommended CTA pair:

- Ürünleri İncele
- Teklif Al

Acceptance:

- User understands the company in 10 seconds.

---

## 7.3 Product Card

Product cards must include:

- Image
- Product title
- 1–2 sentence summary
- Key specs or use case
- CTA

Recommended CTA:

- İncele
- Teklif Al

Card rules:

- Same height where possible
- Clean image ratio
- No excessive text
- CTA always visible

---

## 7.4 “Why Yiğiter” Card

Standard cards:

1. Üretim Gücü
2. Güçlü Sevkiyat
3. İhracat Deneyimi
4. Uzun Vadeli İş Ortaklığı

Each card:

- Icon or simple visual marker
- Short title
- One short explanation

---

## 7.5 CTA Section

CTA sections should appear:

- Homepage middle
- Homepage bottom
- Product page bottom
- Export page top/middle/bottom

CTA examples:

Turkish:

> Ürün gruplarımız için teklif almak ister misiniz?

Buttons:

- Teklif Al
- WhatsApp ile İletişim

English:

> Looking for a reliable door industry supply partner?

Buttons:

- Request Quote
- Contact Export Team

---

## 7.6 Footer

Footer must include:

- Company name
- Address
- Phone
- Email
- WhatsApp
- Request Quote link
- Product links
- Export link
- Catalogue link / QR if available
- Language link

Footer should not repeat unnecessary seller information.

---

# 8. PRODUCT PAGE STANDARD

Each product page should include:

1. Product hero
2. Short explanation
3. Product images
4. Technical specifications
5. Advantages
6. Use cases
7. Related products
8. PDF catalogue/spec sheet
9. Request quote CTA
10. Contact options

---

## 8.1 Specification Display

Use structured tables for specs.

Example fields:

- Product group
- Material
- Thickness
- Dimensions
- Surface
- Usage area
- Packaging
- MOQ if applicable

Avoid long paragraph descriptions for technical details.

---

# 9. EXPORT PAGE STANDARD

Export page must answer practical buyer questions.

Required sections:

1. Export introduction
2. Product groups for export
3. Packaging
4. Container loading
5. Lead time
6. MOQ
7. Private label / custom production
8. Quality and documentation
9. Distributor application
10. Contact export team

Tone:

- Practical
- International
- Clear
- Logistics-aware

Avoid:

- Generic “we export to the world” copy without details

---

# 10. FORMS

## 10.1 Request Quote Form

Fields:

- Name
- Company
- Phone
- Email
- Product group
- Quantity / estimated volume
- Message

Optional:

- Country
- Delivery location
- File upload

Rules:

- Keep form short
- Mobile friendly
- Show confirmation after submission
- Send product context to sales team

---

## 10.2 Distributor Application Form

Fields:

- Country
- Company
- Website
- Contact person
- Email
- Phone
- Sales channel
- Annual volume
- Product interest
- Message

Rules:

- More detailed than quote form
- Designed for serious export/distribution leads

---

# 11. MOBILE RULES

Mobile first screen must show:

- What Yiğiter does
- Main product direction
- Quote CTA
- WhatsApp / phone path

Rules:

- Avoid large empty hero height
- Buttons must be thumb-friendly
- Product cards must stack clearly
- WhatsApp contact should be easy
- No tiny text

---

# 12. SEO AND AI CONTENT RULES

## 12.1 Page Titles

Titles must include:

- Company name
- Product/category
- B2B positioning when possible

Example:

> Yiğiter Orman Ürünleri | Kapı Sanayii Tedarikçisi ve Üretim Çözümleri

---

## 12.2 Meta Descriptions

Descriptions should explain:

- What the page offers
- Main products
- B2B/export direction

---

## 12.3 AI Readability

Each important page should have a visible descriptive paragraph.

Rules:

- Mention company name
- Mention product groups
- Mention B2B customers
- Mention manufacturer/distributor/export role
- Avoid vague marketing copy

---

# 13. ACCESSIBILITY

Minimum requirements:

- Good contrast
- Readable font sizes
- Alt text for important images
- Clear button labels
- Keyboard-friendly forms where possible
- No text embedded only inside images

---

# 14. PERFORMANCE

Rules:

- Compress images before upload
- Use modern formats where possible
- Avoid unnecessarily large hero images
- Lazy load below-the-fold images
- Keep pages lightweight

Image recommendations:

- Hero: optimized large image
- Cards: consistent ratio and compressed
- Product detail: high quality but not oversized

---

# 15. CONTENT DO / DON'T

## Do

- Use real product and factory photos
- Use short B2B-focused text
- Use specs and proof
- Use clear CTAs
- Use verified metrics
- Use Turkish and English pages intentionally

## Don't

- Overload pages with long paragraphs
- Use vague corporate slogans
- Hide contact options
- Use inconsistent product cards
- Mix consumer retail tone with B2B tone
- Publish unverified numbers
