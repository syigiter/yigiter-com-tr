Yiğiter is positioned as:

- Door industry supplier
- Manufacturer
- Distributor
- Export partner
- Long-term B2B supply partner

Core product groups:

- Door surfaces
- Melamine solutions
- Frames
- Casings / architraves / pervaz
- Paint solutions
- Panels
- Door industry components

Do NOT position Yiğiter as:

- Retail seller
- Consumer-facing door shop
- Simple catalogue website

Main brand message:

> Quality, continuity, strong logistics and long-term partnership for the door industry.
